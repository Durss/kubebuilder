What is the purpose of this application?

  • If someday the administrators want to add a new kube to the game, they can look at the most wanted kubes.
    Therefore, the kubes on the podiums have a better chance of being added to the game.

    Creating a kube on this application will not put it in the game. Only the administrators can do that.
    The administrators are free to use this list or not.

Votes and creations :

  • You can vote 3 times each day, and only once per kube.
  • The votes are weighted according to the statistics of the player. The number of zones discovered and number of construction points are taken into account. The higher those numbers, the more that player's vote will count relative to the other player's votes.
  • You can create only 3 kubes per day. This is to prevent abuse and flooding.

Banishment :

  • If you abuse the voting, creation or reporting rights, all of those can and will be irrevocably removed without prior notice.
